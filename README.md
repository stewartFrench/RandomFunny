RandomFunny - Your Pocket Comedian on a Timer

Ever wished someone would randomly interrupt your day with terrible dad jokes? Well, wish granted!

RandomFunny is the app nobody asked for but everyone secretly needs. It speaks eye-rolling jokes at random intervals while you're driving, working, cooking, or pretending to listen to your boss on that Zoom call.

Features that'll make you groan:

  - 183+ hand-picked groaners, puns, and knee-slappers
  - Customizable joke frequency (1 to 120 minutes - we won't judge)
  - Random timing mode for surprise chuckles
  - Works in the background - jokes follow you everywhere
  - Perfect for CarPlay - comedy on the road!
  - No internet required - offline hilarity guaranteed
  - Choose your favorite voice - Daniel (British) is particularly charming

Why you need this:

  - Awkward silence at work? BOOM. Joke.
  - Long commute getting boring? WHAM. Punchline.
  - Forgot how to smile? BAM. Dad joke to the rescue.

Warning: May cause unexpected snort-laughing in public places. Your coworkers will think you've finally lost it. Your passengers will question your life choices. Your dog will give you that look.

Download now and let the randomness begin. Your day is about to get 47% sillier.

Note: App does not actually improve your own joke-telling abilities. That's still on you.

## Privacy Policy

**RandomFunny does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.**

All settings are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. No data is transmitted to external servers. The app does not require an internet connection to function.
